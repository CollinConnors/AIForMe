# Welcome to AI For Me
This website is a free AI course resource designed for anyone interested in learning about artificial intelligence. It includes lecture slides, recorded lessons, homework prompts, readings, and curated references that introduce key ideas behind modern AI systems—especially large language models—and their impact on education, work, and everyday life. The material is accessible to beginners and does not assume a technical background. Whether you are a student, educator, professional, or independent learner looking for an introductory artificial intelligence course, you are welcome to explore the content at your own pace.
